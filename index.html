FAQs

Answers to Your Most Common Questions about Hecht Contact Lenses, Products, and Services

What types of contact lenses do you offer?

We offer a wide range of customized contact lenses, including spherical lenses, toric lenses, multifocal lenses, and special lenses tailored to individual needs.

How can I order customized contact lenses for my patients?

Orders can be placed directly through our secure online portal, or by contacting our dedicated support team via phone or email. We require patient prescription details and specific fitting parameters.

Do you provide resources and support for opticians?

Yes, we offer comprehensive fitting guides, educational webinars, and one-on-one consultation support with our clinical experts to ensure successful fittings and patient satisfaction.

How do I care for my contact lenses?

Proper care involves daily cleaning and disinfection using recommended solutions, following the replacement schedule provided, and never using tap water. Always wash your hands before handling lenses.

Can I wear contact lenses if I have astigmatism?

Absolutely. We offer customized toric contact lenses specifically designed to correct astigmatism, providing clear and stable vision for patients with this condition.

Are your lenses suitable for children?

Our lenses are suitable for children under the guidance of an eye care professional, often used for vision correction or specialized treatments like myopia control.
